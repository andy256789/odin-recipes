Deviled Eggs

Description

These deviled eggs are delicious for Easter brunch and are always popular at our holiday parties. Adjust the amount of salt and sugar in this recipe to suit your taste. I like to add onion and paprika for a little more texture.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-cooked eggs
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped onion
  • Â½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch paprika, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Peel hard-cooked eggs.
  2. Slice eggs in half lengthwise and remove yolks; set whites aside.
  3. Mash yolks with a fork in a small bowl. Stir in mayonnaise, sugar, vinegar, mustard and onion; mix well and season with salt to taste.
  4. Stuff or pipe egg yolk mixture into egg whites.
  5. Sprinkle with paprika. Refrigerate until serving.
  6. Enjoy!
